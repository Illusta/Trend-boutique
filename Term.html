Order Request

Our online process begins with an order request, not an instant purchase. Simply fill out the form specifying your name, e-mail I'd, phone no. and language. measurements will be discussed on the call. A friendly member of our team will then contact you to discuss the details, confirm your order, and answer any questions. Alternatively, you can visit our physical store for a personalized ordering experience.
Design & Fabric Ownership: Please note that we specialize in stitching and tailoring Punjabi suits. To ensure your unique vision comes to life, we require you to provide the design and fabric. We do not offer pre-designed suit options or sell fabrics ourselves.

Payment options

We offer secure payment methods for your convenience:
Online Orders:
A 50% down payment will be processed over the phone during the confirmation call to secure your order. The remaining 50% balance will be due upon delivery of your custom-made suit. We currently accept digital payments only for online orders.
Offline Orders (In-Store):
Cash on Delivery (COD) is available when placing an order at our physical store.

Pricing & Customization

Custom Suit Pricing: Please note that the prices listed on our website are estimates for base suit styles. Since each suit is crafted to your specifications, the final price may vary slightly depending on the chosen design complexity and fabric selection. We will confirm the final pricing during your confirmation call.
Fabric & Style Availability: While we offer a wide range of customization options, some intricate designs or specialty fabrics may require a consultation in-store to ensure feasibility. For online orders, the available fabric options will be clearly indicated during the selection process.

Delivery & Shipping Information

Estimated Delivery Timeframes: Please note that our delivery timelines are estimates and may vary depending on current order volume. We strive to complete the stitching of your custom suit within 2-3 business days. However, shipping times will depend on the chosen service and delivery location. The final estimated delivery date will be confirmed after your suit is stitched.
Shipping Costs: Online orders will incur standard shipping charges based on your chosen delivery service and location. These costs will be reflected during checkout. For in-store orders, there are no additional delivery charges if you choose to pick up your suit directly. However, if you require your in-store order to be shipped, an additional fee will be applied based on your location.

Cancellations and Refunds

Please understand that due to the custom-made nature of our suits, we generally do not accept returns or cancellations. Since the fabric is procured specifically for your order, a cancellation fee may apply if a request is made after 12 hours from placing the order.
However, we are committed to your satisfaction. In the rare instance of physical damage to the suit upon delivery (excluding any fitting issues), we will offer a full refund (minus shipping charges).
Please note that this does not cover situations where the measurements you provide are inaccurate. We take great care to stitch your suit precisely based on the information you give us. In the unlikely event of a mistake on our part with the stitching based on the measurements provided, we will offer a 30% refund of the total amount (excluding shipping charges).But in case if the customer is unable to provide Correct measurements then we will be unable to do anything.

Dispute Resolution & Customer Satisfaction

We prioritize clear communication throughout the order process. You will be informed about all aspects of your order, including pricing, customization details, shipping timelines, and policies.
Should you have any questions or concerns after reviewing your order confirmation or browsing our website, feel free to contact us via our contact form. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
For complex issues, we will prioritize a phone call to discuss the matter directly. For simpler questions, such as order status updates, we will respond via your preferred method (SMS or email) based on the information provided during your order request.
