Trend Boutique Privacy policies

Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly when you use our website, such as: Name: To personalize your experience and identify you during communication.
Phone Number: To contact you regarding your order requests and confirm details.
Email Address : If provided during order requests, your email can be used for order confirmation or communication about your request.
Preferred Language: This can help us tailor website content or communication to your preference (if offered).
We do not collect any browsing data through cookies or analytics tools. Your browsing activity on our website remains private.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:
* To process your order requests and fulfill your custom suit needs.
* To contact you regarding your order, answer questions, and confirm details.
* To improve our website functionality and user experience (if applicable with optional language preference).

Data Sharing

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us with order fulfillment, such as payment processors or delivery companies. We will only share the necessary information required for these purposes (e.g., name and address for delivery).

Data Security

We take steps to secure your information using industry-standard practices. While we don't collect browsing data, any information you provide through forms is protected using encryption methods for sensitive data like phone numbers.

Your Choices

You are not obligated to fill out any forms on our website. Browsing our suit designs and styles does not require any personal information from you. We only collect the data you choose to provide during the order request process to fulfill your custom suit needs and ensure a smooth communication experience.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact form
